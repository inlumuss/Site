The Project

Why In Lumuss?

In Lumuss was born from a conviction that this knowledge deserves to live again.

Every month, we dive deep into a single theme — Hermeticism, sacred geometry, forgotten mythologies, the history of astronomy — and distil it into something tangible and beautiful that arrives at your door.

Each envelope holds a 4x6 art print related to the topic of the month, doubling as wall art or a treasured gift. A handwritten-style letter with curated bibliography, and original stickers or craft drawn from the same symbolic universe.

This is not a subscription to content. It is an invitation to a way of seeing.

The Curator

About Me

Hi! My name is Eli Reyes — a philosophical woman living in Belgium, born in Mexico with deep cultural roots in esoteric and spiritual ritual culture.

I have studies in Business Operations and B2B Marketing, but my dream is to run this mail club to spread the word about occult topics I find very relevant in life. Information is an important tool to get to know ourselves. All they didn't teach us at school!

I love esoteric illustrations and art, so this is my intent for sharing this passion with you — encouraging reflection from all the dusty shelves of second-hand bookshops, libraries and classes in cities I barely remember.

"The beginning of wisdom is the discovery of our own ignorance."
— attributed to William Durant

In Lumuss grew from a desire to share that feeling — to make esoteric and occult history approachable, beautiful, and tangible. I research each theme with care, create the print and write each letter as if I am writing to a close friend, wishing to share my findings and reflection.

The name comes from old Latin: in lux, into the light. Everything here is an attempt to illuminate a corner of the world that has been allowed to grow dark.
